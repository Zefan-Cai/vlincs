Latest regenerated subpart promotion:
The current split-best assignment had predicted_global_id=92 components but
only 83 component_label groups, because the promoted 35/48/20 component split
updated IDs without refreshing labels. A no-op manifest replay refreshed the
component labels, then a no-anchor subpart proposer regenerated candidates
from the current namespace using assignment evidence plus weakmetric/OSNet and
DINOv2 features. The promoted combo applies two split-child repairs:
rank02 moves 14 tracklets from 960000350 to 960000351, and rank09 moves 5
tracklets from 960000350 to 960000480. Direct full improved from
0.661843/0.523137/0.534683 to 0.662638/0.523746/0.534926. The fixed
density_simple + p005_area delivery score improved from
0.664050/0.524893/0.536568 to 0.664835/0.525495/0.536810. Pair proxy for the
combo is 0.782244/0.850111/0.724411. Rank09 is an important hard label for the
admission model: pair F1 decreased slightly on its own, but direct full score
improved, so pair-only ranking is insufficient.

Latest residual split refutation:
After promoting the 3-component split, a residual greedy12 split probe was run
on top of best_combo_split_assignments.csv. The eval-only pair proxy improved
from 0.781330/0.849326/0.723413 to 0.785529/0.862643/0.721070 by adding
smaller splits such as 96000032 SigLIP k=4, 96000009 weakmetric k=6,
96000040 SigLIP k=6, and 96000019 SigLIP k=6. Full-score validation did not
improve: direct full remained 0.661843/0.523137/0.534683, identical to the
promoted 3-component split at 6 decimals. This is now a hard negative for the
split admission model: pair precision gains alone are not enough to predict
pipeline IDF1 gain.

Latest top-single / small-merge refutation:
Top residual single splits were exported and full-scored individually. The six
tested no-anchor single splits, covering component 96000032 variants and
component 96000009 variants, all tied direct full at
0.661843/0.523137/0.534683. The best-looking single, rank009 96000009
weakmetric k=5, also tied delivery after density_simple and p005_area:
0.663955/0.524825/0.536460 and 0.664050/0.524893/0.536568. Current-best edge
diagnostics were rerun with min-size-1 fragment candidates. The no-GT top50
small-merge pool had only 6/50 eval-true edges; top no-GT rank001-rank004,
including the first eval-true singleton merge, all tied direct full at
0.661843/0.523137/0.534683. This closes the naive residual split and naive
targeted re-merge branches.

Latest component-split promotion:
The current rank58 assignment was scored locally without DB by using
kit/demo_data/ds1 tracklet parquets plus DS1 GT downloaded from S3. The scorer
first reproduced the previous direct full score exactly:
0.655836/0.519304/0.534154. A no-anchor split/admission edit then split three
impure components: 96000035 with SigLIP k=2, 96000048 with weakmetric k=4, and
96000020 with weakmetric k=6. Pair F1/precision/recall improved from
0.773980/0.823426/0.730136 to 0.781330/0.849326/0.723413. Direct full improved
to 0.661843/0.523137/0.534683. The existing density_simple wrapper scored
0.663955/0.524825/0.536460, and the canonical p005_area delivery score is the
new best 0.664050/0.524893/0.536568. MCAM04/Tc6 direct IDF1 improved from
0.564208 to 0.577029. An extended greedy split improved pair proxy to 0.784701
but did not improve direct full beyond the 3-component split, so the simpler
3-component split is promoted.

Latest teacher-consensus run:
h100-test-3 teacher-consensus rank58 tested three no-anchor teacher families:
structural-policy variants, subpart-promoted variants, and time-agglomeration
variants. All canonical density_simple + p005_area delivery scores tied the
current best at 0.658025/0.521057/0.536049. No merge edge was accepted:
subpart_promoted rejected 3403/3403 edges by threshold, timeagglom_diverse
rejected 3320 by threshold and 1 by cannot-link conflict, and structural_policy
rejected 3402 by threshold and 1 by teacher condition. This refutes
teacher-consensus merge as a promotion source for the current rank58
neighborhood; the teacher outputs are better used as hard-negative/referee
signals.

direction is targeted component edge diagnostics plus local split/admission
guards on impure components. The edge-diagnostic script was prepared, but
h100-test-2 and h100-test-3 entered stopping state before completion.
